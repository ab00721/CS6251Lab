The Yurts at Pacific Trails

What is a Yurt?
Our luxury yurts are permanent structures four feet off the ground. Each yrut is fully enclosed with canvas walls, a wooden floor, and a roof dome that can be opened.
How are the yurts furnished?
Each yrt is furnished with a queen-size bed with down quilt and gas-fired stove. Your luxury camping experience included electricity and a sink with hot and cold running water. Shower and restroom facilities are located in the lodge.
What should I bring?
Most guests pack comfortable walking shoues and plan to dress for changing weather with light layers of clothing. It's also helpful to bring a flashlight and a sense of adventrue!
